Cookies allow the website or application to recognise you on future visits, and they may also store user preferences and other information. You can read more information about cookies at www.allaboutcookies.org.
    2. Luxe may install cookies on your computer or mobile device to ensure the Services perform in the way you expect, and to improve your overall experience of using our website and applications. You can set your browser or mobile device to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some of our Services may not function properly without cookies.

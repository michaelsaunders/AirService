FAQs for Customers

  • How do I check-in at a venue?

    Simply open the app and either check-in at the location where AirService has located you, or tap "Search Location" to find the venue you're looking for.

  • How do I connect to a venue and start ordering?

    Once you've checked-in at a venue you'll see up the top of the home screen a prompt to connect. Simply tap that and you're good to go!

  • Can I view the menu without connecting to the venue?

    Absolutely. Simply open the app and check-in at the venue. The menu will download to your phone and you'll be able to view it at your leisure. Once you're ready to order simply connect to the venue and start ordering. (see FAQ "How do I start ordering?")

  • How do I re-order a past order?

    When you click on My Orders, you will notice a button at the top right of the screen called 'Past Orders'. From here you can view all your previous orders from the past 24 hours and re-order any of them with just one click.

  • How do I place an order for a later time?

    Place your order as usual, however when you come to the My Order screen you will notice a Place my Order section at the bottom of the order. By default this is set to 'ASAP', however by tapping the arrow, you can set the order time to a different time. Perfect for those moments when you want to plan ahead, perhaps for dinner later that night, or a romantic bottle of champagne to be delivered at that perfect moment.

  • What if there's a problem with my order?

    No problem, simply talk to a member of staff, they can assist you with your order.

  • Why did I receive a message from the venue I'm connected to?

    AirService can be used to send you messages that will improve service for you, like advising that your table is ready. To keep you up to date with the latest offers, AirService venues are also allowed to send all currently connected customers up to 2 messages per hour. These messages may contain special offers or alert you to events that are currently happening in your vicinity.

  • Can I connect to more than one venue at once?

    You can. This means that you can easily move between venues without having to settle your account. For example if you're connected to a hotel while staying there, but also want to connect to a particular bar or restaurant that you're visiting that night.

    See also FAQ "How do I disconnect from a venue?

  • How do I view which venues I'm connected to?

    If you tap on the Settings tab in the app you can view all venues you're currently connected to. You can also view your current order history at each venue.

  • How do I disconnect from a venue?

    You can only disconnect from a venue once you have paid your bill. To pay your bill tap on View Bill on the app home screen and this will bring up your current bill. From there tap "Request checkout to pay" and follow the prompts!

  • How do I add a venue to my favourites list?

    After you have checked-in you will see a small heart at the top of the screen. Tapping this icon will automatically add the venue to your favourites.

  • How do I pre-set the amount I want to spend at a venue?

    You can set your maximum spend alert through the Settings tab on the app. Once you've reached this amount, AirService will alert you that you are now spending beyond your set limit. Of course, you can override it if you want to keep ordering.

  • How do I change my account profile details?

    You can change your account profile details through the Settings tab on the app.

  • How do I change or delete my profile photo?

    You can change or delete your profile photo through the Settings tab on the app. We strongly recommend making sure your profile has a clear picture of you to help venue staff find you to deliver your order!

  • Why do I need push notifications enabled to use AirService

    AirService relies on push notifications to provide its service to you. It needs to send you push notifications to keep you informed about any orders you place.

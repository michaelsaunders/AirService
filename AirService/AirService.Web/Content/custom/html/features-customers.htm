Features for Customers

  • No queues!!

    With AirService your days of waiting in queues are over. Order from wherever you are, and your order will come straight to you. Now that's service.

  • Browse the menu before you arrive

    Haven't decided if this is the venue for you? Check in via AirService and have a look at the menu. You can even do this at home before you leave!!

  • Timed ordering

    So you're sitting on the beach and want to order room service for later... With AirService simply open the app and order away, set your preferred time and when you arrive, food will be ready.

  • Reordering

    Feel like ordering the same again? With AirService it's one-click easy. Simply view your past orders and hit the REORDER button. Your last order will be instantly reordered.

  • Romance

    Feel like ordering your girlfriend a bottle of champagne at midnight without her knowing? Order with AirService earlier in the night using timed ordering.

  • Stay informed

    AirService keeps a running total of how much you've spent, to help eliminate nasty surprises. You can check how much you've spent, and view your past orders, at any time. Suddenly it's much easier to manage your spending, even after a few drinks.

  • Know your limits

    Decided you're on a budget tonight? With AirService you can keep track of the amount you've spent, and even pre-set a spend limit, and when you reach it you'll receive a little warning. Of course, you can keep ordering if you want to!!

  • Eat with your eyes

    A picture says a thousand words, and with AirService you can view an item before you order.

  • Add AirService to your venue

    Experience what AirService can do for your venue and customers. We know you will be impressed.

FAQs for Venues

  • How do I accept a customer's connection request?

    When a customer requests a connection to your venue their name will appear on the left of the venue iPad screen under the "waiting" tab. Simply tap on that customers name, and accept their request. The customer is then free to start ordering!

  • How do I accept an order?

    Tap the green 'yes' tick next to the order to accept it. A yellow paper plane will then appear next to the order indicating it is being prepared for delivery.

  • How do I show an order has been delivered?

    Tap on the yellow paper plane next to the order. That order will then be shown to be "Closed".

  • I accidently accepted an order, how do I undo this?

    Swipe to the right across the order and you'll see. :)

  • Can I delete a customer's order?

    Yes, swipe right on the item they have ordered and click Delete.

  • How do I process a customer's bill?

    In the same way you usually would. We want AirService to be as transparent as possible in your venue, and this means not messing with your existing point of sale systems. Once the customer is done, you process their bill in exactly the same way as you do now.

  • Can I print a customer's order history?

    Yes. You need a compatible AirPrint printer that works with an iPad, and from there simply tapping on the Print Order History button will automatically print the order history for your customer.

  • How do I know where to deliver a customer's order to?

    When the customer connects, they specify their location to the staff member connecting them at a venue iPad. The staff member then enters this information as part of accepting the customer's connection request.

  • How do I change the location to deliver a customer's order to?

    Simply tap on the customer's name and you can edit their location.

  • What does "freezing" a customer do?

    Let's say a customer looks like they need a break from drinking, or is unruly, you can 'freeze' them. This simply puts a block on their app for a set period of time giving you time to send staff to calm them down or politely ask them to leave the venue. AirService has been designed with Responsible Service of Alcohol regulations in mind.

  • How do I send a customer a message?

    Tap on the customer's name, and then tap 'Send Message'.

  • How do I access the administrator level of the app?

    Tap on the cog symbol in the top corner of the iPad app, and enter your admin username and password.

  • What if I've forgotten the administrator password

    Click the 'forgotten password' button and AirService will email you a reminder.

  • How do I update my menus?

    Log in to your AirService account on a computer at www.airservicehq.com/login. From here you will be able to click on Menus and update and add to your menus.

  • Why should I upload pictures of the items in my menus?

    It's been proven that images help sell product far more effectively than words alone. To give your customers the ultimate in AirService ordering in your venue, we highly recommend you take some great shots of your products and add them to the menu. We can even recommend some great photographers in your city.

  • What if I need to take an item temporarily off the menu?

    Easy, you can either login to your AirService account on a computer at www.airservicehq.com/login or the iPad admin and disable items that are not currently available to order.

  • Can I set menus to start and finish automatically?

    Yes, AirService has the ability to schedule menus. Simply login to your AirService account on a computer at www.airservicehq.com/login. From here you will be able to click on Menus and update and schedule them to start and finish automatically.

  • Can I have more than one iPad in my venue?

    Yes. They will all receive the orders at the same time, and the staff with the quickest finger can accept and process the order. You can even have iPads receiving orders only from certain menus.

  • How do I know which iPad has accepted a customer's order?

    The name of the iPad which has accepted the order will appear on the iPad screen next to the order.

  • Can I have different iPads in my venue receiving orders from different menus?

    Yes, for example if you had a bar and a kitchen, all the drink orders could go to one iPad, whilst the food orders would go straight to the kitchen.

  • How do I customize what the iPhone app looks like?

    Login at www.airservicehq.com and you will be able to upload your own images, customise your app, and even add sponsor advertising to AirService's iPhone app that your customers will all see when they check in.

  • Is AirService safe and secure to use in my venue?

    AirService is very safe to use in your venue. All orders are encrypted, and AirService does not interfere with your point of sale systems in any way. Further, you have full control over which customers are able to use AirService in your venue (see the FAQ "How do I ensure my customers pay their bill?")

  • How do I ensure my customers pay their bill?

    Customers cannot place orders unless you have accepted their connection request and they have connected to your venue. For security, we recommend that before accepting a connection request you take the customer's credit card details or other identification details.

    In additions to this, Customers can only be disconnected from your venue from a venue iPad. So unless you are satisfied they have paid their bill they will remain connected to your venue and you will be able to contact them. You retain full control at all times.

  • Can I temporarily turn AirService off in my venue?

    Yes, either from the website at www.airservicehq.com or from the iPad (when logged in as an administrator), simply click the 'switch' to turn AirService on or off.

Features for Venues

  • Customizable app

    Why was huge amounts of time and money testing and maintaining an app from scratch. With AirService you can customise the app to match the rest of your brand and make it truly yours. Choose your colours, select from our recommended backgrounds or upload your own, and add your logo quickly and easily right from the AirService website. You can even editit at any time, and the app will automatically change on all your customers iPhones and iPads.

  • Multiple iPads

    Do you serve from more than one spot at your venue? No problem, with AirService you can split your incoming orders. Want your drinks to go to one bar, and food orders to go to the kitchen? Consider it done.

  • Pick up option

    Don't want to deliver your customer's orders to them? AirService is still working for you. Use the pick up option, and when a customer's order is ready they'll be sent an alert to come pick it up, along with a number to identify their order. Do away with those buzzers that are always breaking down or going missing. Or dedicate an area to receiving and preparing orders so that you have a continual flow of customers just coming past to pick up their orders once they're ready. Remove bottlenecks as customers queue to order and then wait for their order to be prepared, and really maximise the efficiency of your venue!!

  • Advertising

    We've even set aside space on the homepage of the app for you to include some advertising. This can be set to change every day, so you might choose to advertise happy hour on a Monday, include a sponsor's logo on a Tuesday, and advertise a special on a Wednesday. You can even rent this bit of advertising space to another company. The possibilities are endless. Or you can switch this feature off if you'd rather do without.

  • Get creative with your menus

    AirService allows you to really draw your customers in with your menus. Upload beautiful photos of your goods. Include a description if you like, maybe nutritional details for food? Plus, AirService isn't for just food and drinks. Anything that can be listed can be offered through AirService. Massages, in-house movies, reservations, let your imagination run free...

  • Timed menus

    Does your venue have more than one menu which run at different times? Like a breakfast menu, lunch menu and dinner menu? Or a special happy hour menu that only runs on Tuesdays at 6pm? With AirService it's not a problem. Upload your menus and set the times for each. AirService will automatically stop and start the menus at the correct time, so your customers see the correct menu without any hassle for you.

  • Special offers

    Add a specials menu to make sure your customers see what's on offer at all times. This will be highlighted in the menu to help catch their attention.

  • Easy menu updates

    Feel like changing your menu but don't want to have to re-print all your material? With AirService it's a concern of the past. Simply and easily edit your menus on the website, and they're instantly updated across any and all your customers devices.

  • Better understand your venue

    AirService offers a great range of analytical tools to better understand the ordering habits of your customers. See that they usually order a pina colada from the pool bar, but hardly ever order a beer at the same time as a cheese sandwich.

  • Message customers

    Want to request that a specific customer speaks to a member of staff? Or want to send a message to all the customers currently using AirService in your venue? AirService allows you to do both, quickly and easily.

  • Customers had too much?

    Worried that someone might be re-ordering drinks a little too quickly? Simply tap the FREEZE button next to the user's name, and temporarily put a stop on their orders for a preset amount of time. Of course this can also be cancelled at any time.

  • That oh-so-valuable social media exposure

    AirService helps promote your venue by encouraging your customers to post to Facebook and Twitter to let their friends and followers know where they are and how much they're enjoying it.
